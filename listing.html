About this tile saw

Professional-grade MK Diamond 10-inch wet saw — the one you'd see on a real job site. Handles porcelain, ceramic, travertine, and natural stone up to 3.5" thick. The water tray catches slurry so your garage stays clean, and the continuous-rim diamond blade leaves polished cuts that don't need finishing.

I've had this saw for three years and used it for two of my own bathroom remodels plus a patio job. Blade was replaced six months ago — still plenty of life. Comes fully tuned, with a 25-ft GFCI extension cord so you can run it right off a regular outlet.

Great for: backsplash tile, bathroom floors, shower surrounds, outdoor patio stone, fireplace surrounds. Not recommended for: glass tile (needs a different blade) or anything over 4" thick.

What's included

  • Continuous-rim diamond blade (≈60% life)
  • 25-ft 12-gauge GFCI extension cord
  • Water pump + drainage hose
